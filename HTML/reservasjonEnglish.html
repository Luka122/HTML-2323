Book a table at Flora Norway

Flora Norway is a two story nature themed restaurant that has the ocean as its neighbour and the city center close by, perfect for a walk around the city after a meal. The restaurant can seat 40 people and has 15 tables which can be moved to fit larger groups.

The food is made with the best ingredients available on the market. If you stop by for lunch, you can get a a large variety of drinks, meals and desserts. To see our menu press or click the menu button in the navigation bar.
To book a table please contact us through any of the provided contact information.
