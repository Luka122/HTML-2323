Flora Norway

Welcome to Flora Norway. The restaurant spans two floors floors, with large windows out towards the pier outside. The outdoor dining area has a fantastic location in the city with a view of the fjord and Akershus Fortress. On hot summer days, this is the perfect place to eat outside, with a view to the sea and that teeming life.
